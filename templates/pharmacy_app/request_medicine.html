How It Works

Request Process:
  1. Fill out the request form with medicine details
  2. Submit your request to the pharmacy
  3. Admin will review and process your request
  4. You'll be notified of the status
Important Notes:
  • Provide accurate medicine name
  • Specify realistic quantity needed
  • Requests are processed within 24-48 hours
  • You can track request status in your dashboard
Benefits:
  • Get notified when medicine becomes available
  • Help pharmacy stock popular medicines
  • Priority service for regular customers
  • Flexible delivery options
